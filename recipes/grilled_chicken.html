Rosemary Lemon Grilled Chicken

This simple, sensational marinade and sauce for grilled chicken is made with lemon, garlic, rosemary, and butter. Separate the marinade into thirds: 1/3 for marinating, 1/3 for basting, and 1/3 for topping.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup fresh rosemary
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 6 ounce boneless chicken
  • slat and pepper

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the rosemary and garlic, and cook for 2 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and let cool slightly. Stir in the lemon juice.
  3. Place the chicken in a large resealable plastic bag. Pour 1/3 of the marinade over the chicken, seal the bag, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Remove chicken from marinade and discard marinade.
  5. Grill chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  6. Baste with remaining marinade during grilling.
  7. Serve with the remaining marinade on the side.
