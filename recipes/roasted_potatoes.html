Roasted Red Potatoes

Roasted red potatoes at their best — plain and simple. Red potatoes are tossed with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roasted to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds red potatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Wash and cut the potatoes into quarters.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the potatoes, olive oil, salt, pepper, and spices. Toss to coat.
  4. Spread the potatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  5. Roast for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and tender.
  6. Serve hot as a side dish.
